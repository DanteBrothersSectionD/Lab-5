Sythesizers

Synthesizers are electronic instruments that generate audio signals, allowing musicians to create a wide range of sounds by manipulating waveforms, filters, and effects

Drum Machines

Drum machines are electronic devices that simulate drum sounds and rhythms, allowing musicians and producers to create intricate beats and percussion patterns without the need for live drummers

Eletric Guitar

Electric guitars are stringed instruments that use electromagnetic pickups to convert string vibrations into electrical signals, allowing for a wide range of sounds and effects
